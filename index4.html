Logo

The name of the brand Amealy is the short abbreviation of A+ meal. Amealy uses Futura font to give viewers modern atmosphere. The house on the logo means we make customers feel home in our restaurant by food and customer service. Amealy is offering the best food in 3 dollars you can get from because Amealy's food has no profit margin. We don't gain any income out of the food. The purpose of the brand is preventing people from eating Mcdonalds 1 dollar menus because they are cheap. We take the responsibility of people's fast and healty lunch and dinner.

Signage

Amealy chose the color of red for logo and signage as well. The color red symbolizes the exciting, love, and passion. It is also one of the most striking color. Since the most important job for Amealy is advertising, we need a striking color that attracts people.

Menu

The main concept of Amealy's food is no profit margin. We provides a burger, salad, and pasta, which is 3 dollars each and consider people's eating habitant. People have options to choose different kinds of meat, fruit, and pasta. We also provides beverage, which doesn't have profit margin as well. People can enjoy their meal with healthy and tasty food without any concerns about money.
