Jason Statham

Jason Statham is a movie star, who has a long career with action movies. In almost every movie, he shows his action in his own way. He is always a main character, or he is in a main character's side as an assistent in the movie.

Statham Card Deck

The Statham Card Deck is composed of 24 different cards of Jason Statham as different characters from 24 different movies.

Composition

On the front side of the card, there is a picture with a name of the character and Stathamness. Stathamness is how valuable he is. More Stathamness means more valuable. On the back side, there are a name of the movie, job, value, and stats. Every card has four different values even though some cards share same values. Since his roles in the movies are similar, his stats are all same in 24 different cards.

Stathamness

Stathamness is the most significant component since it determines how valuable it is. More Stathamness means more valueable. However, there is an exception. There is an Unique Stathamness, which only two cards have it out of 24. It makes the card the most unique and strongest Jason Statham.
