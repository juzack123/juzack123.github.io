How to use Mouth Guard

First, buy a mouth guard in the martial art store nearby.

Second, boil the water and put the mouth guard in the boiled water.

Third, take the mouth guard out off the boiled water after 30 seconds.

Fourth, put it in your mouth and bit it for 30 seconds.

How to take off Eye contact Lenses

First, put your eye drop water into your eyes and wait for 5 minutes.

Second, hold your finger on the edge of the eye contact.

Third, while holding it, use the other hand's thumb to take it out.
